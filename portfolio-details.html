Product information

  • Category: Mini drone parrot
  • titel: Parot Mambo
  • price: 1299 euro

This is description of product :

The Mambo features a unique modular Smart Block design, which allows you to add optional accessories to the body, such as an FPV camera, toy cannon, and more (each sold separately). The Mambo Fly also supports the Parrot Flypad controller, which can extend your control range up to 328'. The Mambo's 550mAH flight battery can be charged via the included USB cable. Depending on accessories and the environment, the battery can last up to 10 minutes.
